About us


Morep Lda. Is active in the market since 2003. During the16-year activity we developed from a small, unknown firm into a company with stores spread over 6 provinces, that is, a leader in the market of telephony enterprises in Mozambique..

In the year 2007 Vodacom engaged Morep lda as their service provider and by2018 we have already established 26 stores in strategic locations to reduce the travel distance of our customers for Vodacom services ,we also coupled it with M-Pesa services to ease and facilitate customer payment .


We were awarded the sole distributorship in Mozambique of SAMSUNG TELEPHONY products in 2011, by 2013 we attained a turnover of USD 12 million. Due to the Currency crunch in the year 2014 like all other business entities we endured a downturn in turn over, but, on the other hand, unlike others, during that very period we increased the number of our stores fro 5 to 26 stores between 2015 to 2018.


Today with a total of 28 stores stores and 160 employees working with us. We are in close discussion with Samsung to embark on an ambitious plan to expand nationwide with an increase of about 20 stores per year. We render our services each day to about 5500 to 6000 customers located in Maputo, Nampula, Tete, Sofala , Manica and Pemba. We have a data base of nearly 100,000 individual customers . Each of our client is handled individually to their satisfaction. We are ready to prepare an offer specially tailored to customer needs and expectations. Apart from the basic array of services we are ready to satisfy even the most specific expectations of our Clients to the utmost possible.

Vision: Our vision is to be known for our brand products and to be the leading and preferred cell phone distribution store nationwide, that provides easy access to quality products.
Mission: In order to achieve our vision, we intend to remain proactive in the business by:

Coming up with innovative plans for our valued customers,

Improve our services and ensure they are competitive in terms of prices and quality,

Timeliness with the highest standards of help in individual approach to every Client ,

Adjusting our offers to Clients' current needs and

Pepping up our stores with new decor and events intended to excite our customers whilst also giving us an edge over our competing Brands.
